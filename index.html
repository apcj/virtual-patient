Case 1.1

What brought you into hospital today?

I’ve been having this really bad pain in my chest.

Could you tell me a bit more about the pain?

It woke me up this morning at about 6am. I got up, walked around at bit…hoping it would settle down, but nothing I do seems to help. I tried taking some gaviscon, but that hasn’t done anything. I’m really worried it might be something serious – I’ve not had anything like this before.

Can you describe it for me?

I can’t really describe it… It just feels like tightness, like a weight resting on my chest.

Any sharp pains when you breathe in or burning pain?

No, it’s no worse when I breathe in and I wouldn’t say it was burning at all. I do get acid reflux from time to time, which feels like that, but that’s nothing like this.

Does the pain go anywhere else?

Yes, I can feel it in my left arm too.

Have you noticed any other symptoms?

I feel a bit short of puff at the moment, but I think that’s just ‘cos of the pain. I do feel a bit sick, but I haven’t been sick.

Have you had any cough or fever?

No I’ve not had any cough. I haven’t checked my temperature, but I haven’t been feeling hot.

Have you had any other medical problems before?

I’m generally pretty healthy – I haven’t had any medical problems or ever been in hospital before.

Do you take any regular medication?

I’m on some tablets for my blood pressure, but I haven’t any problems with it – well, my GP hasn’t mentioned its been too high.

Do you know what it is called?

Amlodopine, I think – is that how you say it? Ten milligrams. Anything other medications? Oh, I do take simvastatin too, I have had some high cholesterol levels. Anything else? No, nothing else.

Are you diabetic or ever been told you have high blood sugar?

My GP did mention my blood sugar was borderline before - I’ve had some blood tests, but I don’t take anything for it.

Do you have any family history of heart problems at all?

My mum had some heart problems in her later years, and she died of a heart attack when she was 90. My brother has angina too – that’s my younger brother. My older brother is fine. Can I ask how old your younger brother is? He’s 54

Do you smoke?

Yeah, I’ve been trying to quit, I know it’s bad for me…I have cut down recently – I probably smoke about 15 a day? And do you drink alcohol? Yes I do drink but not much – maybe a beer or 2 a week. Have you ever taken any recreational drugs? No I’ve never taken recreational drugs.
